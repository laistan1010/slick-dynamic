Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Slick Dynamic Tech ("we", "us", "our") provides website, AI automation and CRM services to businesses. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit this website or contact us, how we use it, and what rights you have over it.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through the contact form on this website, namely:

  • Your full name
  • Your business email address
  • The service you are interested in

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party scripts to monitor your behaviour on this website. We do not collect any information automatically beyond what is required for the website to function (e.g. standard server logs maintained by our hosting provider).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and prepare a tailored proposal
  • Communicate with you about the services you have requested
  • Maintain records of our business relationship, where one is established

We do not use your information for unrelated marketing purposes without your separate, explicit consent.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share limited information with the following categories of service providers, strictly for the purpose of delivering our services to you:

  • Payment processing: if you become a client, we use Stripe to issue invoices and process payments. Stripe receives only the information necessary to process your payment, in accordance with Stripe's own privacy policy.
  • Communication tools: email or messaging platforms used to correspond with you directly.

We will only disclose your information beyond these circumstances if required to do so by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain enquiry information for as long as is reasonably necessary to respond to you and, where a business relationship is established, for the duration of that relationship plus any period required by applicable tax or accounting regulations. You may request deletion of your information at any time, as described in Section 7 below.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to our processing of your data at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 9 below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational or regulatory reasons. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out via the contact form on our homepage.
